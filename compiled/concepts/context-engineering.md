Context engineering is the practice of dynamically assembling and managing information relevant to an AI agent's operations, ensuring effective coordination between perception, decision-making, and refinement processes, and utilizing standards like the Model Context Protocol (MCP) for interoperability. This discipline enhances both short-term session management and long-term memory retention, crucial for maintaining the agent’s performance, robustness, and safety in various operational contexts.
